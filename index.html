BrunchBox

Build your profressional network the real way, the human way.

What do you mean?

Make new connections, the real way.

As professionals we have to build our network of people and meet other professionals outside our existing network. When it comes to business, professionals prefer to meet other professionals in person, over a meal.

Brunchbox encourages people to create a new connection the real way. It is a smart tool that connects geographically close professionals, based on their interests, over breakfast or brunch.
